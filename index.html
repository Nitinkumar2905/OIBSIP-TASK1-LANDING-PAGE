Choose Your Dance Style

All types of dance styles all over the world

Bollywood

Learn to move and groove on bollywood songs from our talented and experienced choreographers

Contemporary

Connect your mind and body through fluid dance movements from graceful contemporary dance.

Hip-hop

Learn a wide range of street style dance forms such as locking, popping ,breaking etc. from our choreographers.

Salsa

Build some chemistry and have fun by learning the art of salsa under the guidance of our experts.

Ballet

Classical dance form demanding grace and precision and employing formalized steps and gestures set in intricate, flowing patterns to create expression through movement.

Line Dance

Dance to country music in which dancers line up in a row without partners and follow a choreographed pattern of steps.

Folk Dance

Dance that reflects the life of the people of a certain country or region. Not all ethnic dances are folk dances.

Jazz

Performance dance and style that arose in the United States in the mid 20th century. Jazz dance may allude to vernacular jazz about to Broadway or dramatic jazz.

Ballroom

Ballroom dance is a set of partner dances, which are enjoyed both socially and competitively around the world.
